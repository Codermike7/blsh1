Cancellation/Rescheduling Policy

Blushing Dolls Concierge maintains a FIRM "NO REFUND" policy. This is due to us scheduling for skilled nursing care, massages, travel accommodations, etc. There are many factors involved in the Blushing Dolls experience, leaving many affected by your cancellation. Deposits are nonrefundable at any time. Rescheduling of appointments must be requested at least 72 hours before scheduled appointment date and be within 6 months of original service date. Reschedules will be based on availability. If you need to cancel/reschedule your services for any reason, contact us at 754-229-9948 or info@blushingdolls.com.
